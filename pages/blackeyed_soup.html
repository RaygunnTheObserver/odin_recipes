Black-eyed Pea Soup

Winter warmth black-eyed bean soup

Ingredients

  • Spices

    • garlic
    • smoked paprika
    • bay leaf
    • thyme
    • salt
    • pepper

  • Vegetables

    • carrots
    • celery
    • leek
    • onion
    • spinach

  • Base Ingredients

    • olive oil
    • black-eyed peas, 2.5 cups, soaked
    • vegetable broth, 4(?) cups
    • diced tomatoes, 1 can
    • barley (experimental)
    • buns or other bread to serve with butter

Steps

  1. soak beans overnight
  2. sautee carrots & celery in a pan (or the sautee setting on the pressure cooker)
  3. add them to the pressure pot, along with onion, broth, beans, canned tomatoes, and spices
  4. Set instant pot to manual/high pressure for 10 minutes
  5. allow to naturally depressurize for 20 minutes, then quick release pressure
  6. Stir in spinach. Residual heat from soup will wilt it. Set the pot to sautee if there is not enough heat
  7. serve
