Ingredients

  • Coating

    • rosemary
    • seasoning salt
    • garlic powder?
    • olive oil
    • salt
    • pepper

  • Vegetables (for two pans)

    • 18 potatoes
    • 6 carrots
    • 6 stalks celery

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. chop carrots and celery into pans first, then potatoes, evenly distributed according to pan size
  3. once pans are full, dump one pan into a mixing bowl
  4. mix coating ingredients into mixing bowl with potatoes etc.
  5. pour from bowl back into pan. Repeat steps 2&3 with other pan
  6. stick both pans in the oven for 70 mins. Stir twice at 45 and 20 minutes
  7. allow potatoes to cool for 10 minutes before eating
