Italian Pasta Sauce

Hearty Tomato Vegetable Pasta Sauce

This is a recipe I've just kind of intuitively improvised over time and without reference to anything.

It's a great dish for meal prep, since it's generally only half the work to make a double batch that lasts in the fridge all week. I basically size the ingredients list proportionally to the size of the largest pot at my disposal

Ingredients

    • spices
    • basil
    • oregano
    • parsley
    • garlic
    • more garlic
    • salt
  • green lentils
  • butter
  • mushroom
  • carrot
  • celery
  • yellow bell pepper
  • crushed tomatoes
  • diced tomatoes
  • zucchini
  • spinach

Steps

  1. add lentils to a pot and boil until tender
  2. heat olive oil in the largest friggin' pot you have in preparation to sauté some vegetables
  3. sauté mushrooms in a pan with butter and garlic. Set aside when done, but not before tasting one and letting your eyes roll back into your skull in pure savory ecstacy
  4. add chopped carrots and celery to olive oil and sauté for a few min
  5. add chopped yellow pepper to the sauté mix
  6. add crushed and diced tomatoes to the sauté mix and stir
  7. chop zucchini and any other desired vegetables and add to tomato mix. Add the mushrooms to the mix as well.
  8. add basil, oregano, parsley, garlic, and salt, then stir well
  9. taste it cause that shiz be boppin with taste bud rizz homie unless it's not, then add more of whatever it needs, you got this
  10. If it tastes delicious now, add like 6 handfuls of spinachmaybe not all at once and stir until wilted
  11. Serve onto a plate of baby shells. This recipe will not taste good with any other type of pasta.
