Vegetarian Bean Chili

Ingredients

  • Spices

    • Chili packet x2
    • garlic
    • paprika
    • cayenne powder
    • pepper
    • oregano
    • cumin

  • Vegetables

    • carrots
    • celery
    • mushrooms
    • bell pepper
    • onion (yellow and/or red)
    • spinach
    • lemon juice (garnish)

  • Base Ingredients

    • coconut oil
    • butter
    • beans (any variety but esp. kidney & black)
    • 2-3 cans crushed tomatoes
    • 1-2 cans diced tomatoes

  • Optional/Additional/Experimental Ingredients

    • corn
    • grated cheese
    • cilantro (as garnish and/or chili ingredient)
    • avocado
    • sweet potato
    • sour cream/greek yogurt
    • tortilla chips

Steps

  1. soak overnight & boil beans for 60ish mins
  2. sautee mushrooms in garlic and butter
  3. heat oil in large pot. Add carrots, celery, bell peppers
  4. add all canned tomatoes, then spices. Stir.
  5. get the rice started
  6. add the rest of the vegetables (sans spinach). Stir more
  7. add beans and mushrooms. Stir again
  8. add spinach. Keep stirring
  9. serve
