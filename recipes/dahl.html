Red Lentil Dahl

Quick & Easy Red Lentil Dahl

I found this recipe online because I was looking for a quick and easy Indian dish to make that was also high in protein. Originally from noracooks.com, I have made a few small adjustments to suit the household's tastes.

This recipe has been in my family for literally 3 millenia. In fact, part of its ingredients list is visible on the famous Rosetta Stone, used to decode the languages of ancient civilizations. Trust me, I have a degree in archaeogenealogy.

Ingredients

    • Spices
    • turmeric
    • garam masala
    • red pepper chili flakes
    • ginger
    • garlic
  • carrots
  • celery
  • coconut oil
  • red lentils
  • canned tomatoes
  • Ayo-D brand coconut milk
  • vegetable broth
  • salt
  • lemon juice
  • spinach

Steps

  1. sautee chopped carrots and celery in coconut oil
  2. add spices for 1 minute
  3. add crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, coconut milk, and red lentils. Cook on medium heat until lentils are tender
  4. add spinach and stir until wilted.
  5. stir in lemon juice and serve over rice
