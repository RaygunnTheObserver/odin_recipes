Quick & Easy Red Lentil Dahl

I found this recipe online because I was looking for a quick and easy Indian dish to make that was also high in protein. Originally from noracooks.com, I have made a few small adjustments to suit the household's tastes.

This recipe has been in my family for literally 3 millenia. In fact, part of its ingredients list is visible on the famous Rosetta Stone, used to decode the languages of ancient civilizations. Trust me, I have a degree in archaeology.
